Overview

• I am currently Changjiang Scholar Distinguished Professor with the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University (SDU), Jinan, China. I studied for my Ph.D in Electrical Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) from August 2006 to December 2010. I previously worked as a Postdoc scholar at University of California, Berkeley (UC Berkeley) from January 2011 to January 2012.
• I served as the chair or steering committee member of many famous international conferences such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ICIP, ROBIO, ICIA, ICAL, etc. I am also the reviewer of top international journals such as TPAMI, TIP, PR, TMM, TCSVT, SPL, SPIC, VCIR, etc. I served as deputy director of China Image and Graphics Youth Working Committee, member of Pattern Recognition and Machine Intelligence Committee and Computer Vision Committee.
• My representative papers have been published in IEEE TPAMI, TRO, TNNLS, TIP, TCYB, NeurIPS, CVPR, ICCV, IJCAI, AAAI, CoRL, ICRA and other authoritative journals and conferences in the field of artificial intelligence and robotics, and more than 20 invention patents have been authorized by China, the United States, Japan and other countries. I won the second prize of Natural Science in Shandong Province and the second prize of Natural Science in China Society of Image and Graphics.
